# 🚀 LearnBits (Pankaj OS) — Ultimate Productivity Tracker

LearnBits is a high-performance, aesthetically pleasing productivity dashboard designed to help you stay on top of your daily routine. With a focus on flow, visual feedback, and streak maintenance, it transforms your "To-Do" list into a dynamic experience.

## 🧠 Motivation & Philosophy

In an era of endless digital distractions, staying focused on a structured routine is a challenge. **LearnBits** (Pankaj OS) is built on the principle of **"Atomic Progress"**. It's not just about listing tasks; it's about:

- **Flow State Optimization**: By providing a dedicated "Timer Hero" for the active task, it minimizes context switching and encourages deep work.
- **Visual Gratification**: Every completed task provides immediate visual feedback through liquid animations and changing progress rings.
- **Consistency over Intensity**: The streak system (Workout, Java, DSA) gamifies long-term habits, ensuring you show up every day.
- **Total Privacy**: Everything runs locally in your browser. Your data stays on your device unless you choose to export it.

## ✨ Key Features

### 🕒 Smart Execution

- **Dynamic Timer Hero**: A central countdown for your active task. It calculates real-time duration (even if the browser is throttled) and alerts you when you're in overtime.
- **Interactive Timeline**: A vertical spine that visualizes your day's progress. Completed tasks are marked with a pulse effect.
- **Activity Log**: A live stream of your actions, tracking exactly when you finished or skipped tasks.

### 📊 Advanced Analytics
- **Completion Donut**: A high-level view of your daily productivity percentage.
- **Category Breakdown**: Specific progress bars for different life segments (Morning, Study, College, etc.).
- **Hero Stats**: At-a-glance counters for Done, Skipped, Left, and Critical tasks.

### 🔥 Momentum & Streaks
- **Goal Tracking**: Dedicated counters for long-term consistency (e.g., Workout Streak, Java Learning, DSA Daily).
- **Burn-in System**: Automatic streak increments upon completing specific task types.

### 🛠️ Personalization
- **Routine Editor**: Add, remove, or modify tasks directly within the app. Configure time, duration, and criticality.
- **Theme Engine**: Seamlessly switch between a sleek **Dark Mode** 🌙 and a crisp **Light Mode** ☀️.
- **Data Portability**: Full support for JSON Export/Import. Back up your routine or move it across devices instantly.

### 🎨 Premium Aesthetics
- **Liquid Background**: A dynamic, glassmorphic "blob" background that reacts to your interaction.
- **Analog Clock Widget**: A refined time-keeping component for professional time management.
- **Inclusive Design**: ARIA-labeled buttons and keyboard-friendly navigation.
